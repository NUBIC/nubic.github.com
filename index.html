NUBIC is a team dedicated to creating web applications and software tools expressly for clinical and translational research at Northwestern.

NUBIC has four goals:

  1. Provide leadership, governance and coordination to leverage and extend Northwestern’s IT infrastructure and research
  2. Share clinical information with Northwestern’s clinical partners in a secure environment
  3. Support basic scientists and clinical and translational researchers with access to a centralized computational infrastructure and database and software development expertise
  4. Train the medical and research staff, medical students, fellows and residents and next generation of biomedical informatics researchers through existing and newly developed programs
